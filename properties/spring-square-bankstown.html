Those two structural shifts plus affordability give Spring Square growing student and professional demand at a relatively higher ~4.0% yield. NAC reads it as an infrastructure-and-yield growth play: freehold AUD title plus the Metro and campus underpin the case. Pricing here is a NAC estimate pending the developer price list.
